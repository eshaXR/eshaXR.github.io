Signal pipeline — key concepts

2-down-1-up staircase

Two correct responses in a row → SOA decreases (harder). One wrong response → SOA increases (easier). Converges on the 70.7% correct threshold — the PSE.

AOT vs PSE

AOT (Audio-Visual Order Threshold) is the minimum SOA at which participants reliably judge order. PSE is the SOA at which they're at chance — perceiving audio and visual as simultaneous.

Why 3 machines?

Deterministic timing requires separation of concerns. Unity controls the staircase and visuals. Max/MSP handles sample-accurate audio scheduling. Dante provides sub-millisecond network audio transport.
